
CHAMA TRUST ‚Äì GROUP FINANCE & MERRY-GO-ROUND MANAGEMENT APP

Chama Trust is a powerful Merry-Go-Round (MGD) & Group Savings Management App that helps self-help groups run their operations efficiently, transparently, and fairly.

It automates contributions, savings, lottery beneficiary selection, reporting, welfare pockets, penalties, expenses, and much more.
